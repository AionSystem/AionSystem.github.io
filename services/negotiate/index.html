The Pipeline

01
📋
Intake
Client uploads requirements, budget range, and timeline. You upload ADA (scope of work). AI extracts structured terms from both sides — no forms, no friction.
PDF.js Claude 3.5 Sonnet Structured Extraction
02
🤖
AI-Mediated Negotiation
AI identifies gaps between client wants and your ADA. Generates compromise proposals. Both sides approve, reject, or counter — asynchronously. No phone calls. No Zoom. Pure document negotiation.
Gap Analysis Proposal Generation Asynchronous
03
📄
Contract Generation
AI drafts full contract from negotiated terms. Includes scope, payment schedule, IP rights, and NDA if required. Both sides review final draft before signing.
Template System Legal Clause Library AI Drafting
04
✍️
Digital Signature + STP Seal
Both parties digitally sign. Contract receives STP seal — SHA-256 bound, timestamped, immutable. Both parties receive signed copies immediately.
DocuSign API Crypto Wallets STP GitHub Ledger
05
🔏
Notarization + Verification
Each party gets contract notarized independently. Uploads notarized copy back to platform by deadline. AI scans document to verify only change is notary stamp/signature.
Computer Vision PDF Diff Verification Scoring
06
🔐
Final Seal
Verified notarized copy receives FINAL STP seal. Contract is now fully executed, verified, and permanently recorded. Audit trail complete.
STP Final Seal Audit Trail Supabase Log
