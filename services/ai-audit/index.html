Public Formula · All Certified Organizations
EpistemicDebt Score
0–100. Public formula. No black box.

Every certified organization receives an Epistemic Debt Score — a 0–100 metric measuring AI epistemic integrity across five components. The formula is public. You can read the spec, audit the computation, and verify the score independently. A score is not a pass/fail. It is a measure of how much unacknowledged epistemic liability your AI deployment carries.

Epistemic Debt Score — EDS Formula
EDS = f(FC, RC, VQ, HR, TT)
Full computation: EPISTEMIC-DEBT-SCORE.md · Five components · Weighted composite · 0–100 scale
FC
Failure Capture
Are failures being logged immutably before remediation begins?
RC
Remediation Clarity
Is the remediation path transparent and traceable against the original failure?
VQ
Verification Quality
Is output verification systematic — or spot-checked and undocumented?
HR
Human Review
Where is human oversight applied and is it documented with the outcome?
TT
Temporal Traceability
Can you prove when each failure was logged — before or after its remediation?
