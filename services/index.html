Custom AION Stack · Framework Design

FrameworkEngineering

Built through failure events. Not theory.
Certainty Scoring Failure Architecture Epistemic Design AI Governance

The AION stack was not designed from first principles in a comfortable room. It was built through live failure events — each framework specifying the exact boundary where AI confidence breaks down.

Framework engineering for your system means mapping your specific failure modes against the AION methodology, then building falsifiable, versioned, convergence-tracked frameworks for your deployment context. Every framework can produce the output "this score is invalid." That is not a bug. That is the point.
