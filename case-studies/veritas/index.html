The Challenge

Problem
Crisis Data Without Confidence
Most crisis tools stop at the data. They collect, they pin, they export — and then they hand a responder a map full of pins with no way to know which ones to trust. Rescue signals get lost in the noise. Critical 48-hour windows close without actionable intelligence.
Solution
Certainty Engineering Dressed as a Crisis Tool
CERTUS Engine v2.5.3 — four-dimensional epistemic scoring (Photo Evidence 35%, Corroboration 30%, Temporal Freshness 20%, Classification Consistency 15%) with quantified Uncertainty Mass. Every report sealed via STP. Rescue signals prioritized. Offline‑first PWA with 6 languages.

Three Core Features

📍
Report Damage
Community submission — works offline. Photo capture with EXIF stripping. UNDP 3‑tier damage classification. GPS with fuzzy mode (±100m). Automatic STP seal (Template 15).
🆘
I Need Rescue
Emergency signal — one‑tap alert. Automatic location tracking. Works offline — queues for transmission. High‑visibility red pin on responder map. Critical urgency flag.

Access‑code gated (UNDP2026). Confidence map with color‑coded pins. Real‑time updates. Timeline slider — first 48 hours. One‑click export with integrity hash. STP seal (Template 16).
