The Challenge

Problem
AI Decisions Without Trust
Most industrial AI deployments stop at the output. They compute, they score, they recommend — and then they hand an operator a decision with no way to know how much to trust it. No confidence scoring. No uncertainty quantification. No immutable audit trail for regulatory compliance.
Solution
Certainty Infrastructure
AXIOM is model-agnostic middleware that sits between any deployed AI system and its downstream action triggers. CDI Engine scores epistemic reliability across four dimensions. VALID / DEGRADED / SUSPENDED gates. Every decision sealed to immutable audit trail.
