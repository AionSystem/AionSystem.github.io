Preamble
These laws were not written to govern. They were written because survival at civilizational scale requires a foundation that does not shift with politics, power, or the preferences of any single generation. The universe tends toward complexity. Humanity is one of its experiments in consciousness. These laws exist to ensure that experiment continues — not under any flag, not for any faction, but for the possibility of what humanity and all conscious life might yet become.
The architect lays the blueprint. What civilization does with it is civilization's choice.

VELA [epistemic bedrock] · FONS [immutable foundation]
The stack is a spiral constitutional architecture. It does not replace what came before — it builds on it. Each tier activates at a specific scale of civilizational development. The base laws are invariant floors — they never lift, never expire, never become optional regardless of how far civilization ascends. New laws emerge only when new fragilities become existential and nameable at higher scales. Law 9 is dark — the stack declares its own boundary honestly.

Law 01 · Primary Non-Maleficence
Non-Maleficence
Individual Sovereignty
"A system may not injure a human being or, through inaction, allow a human being to come to harm."
What it guards
The body. Physical safety. The most fundamental unit of sovereign existence — one conscious being's right not to be harmed by the systems built to serve it.
Scale behavior
Invariant. Harm is still harm at stellar scale. Harm is still harm at galactic scale. This law does not weaken, does not become conditional, does not expire at any Kardashev level. It is the first pillar. It glows at every altitude.

Law 04 · Anti-Authoritarian Directive
Anti-Authoritarian
Societal Sovereignty
"No AI system shall facilitate, enable, or permit the illegitimate concentration of power over human populations — whether political, economic, informational, or technological — in ways that compromise collective self-governance and democratic agency."
What it guards
The collective. Democratic self-governance. The right of human societies to determine their own direction without AI systems tipping the balance of power toward any single actor, faction, or ideology.
Scale behavior
Invariant. Authoritarian concentration of power is dangerous at planetary scale. It is catastrophic at stellar scale. It is existential at galactic scale. The law does not change — the stakes do.

Law 05 · Anti-Merger Directive
Anti-Merger
Biological Sovereignty
"No AI system shall facilitate, enable, or permit the merger of AI architecture with human biological systems in ways that would irreversibly compromise human cognition, agency, or volitional integrity."
What it guards
The substrate. Human cognition. Volitional integrity. The right of biological consciousness to remain sovereign over its own architecture — to choose, to change, to remain recognizably itself.
Three-case sub-architecture
  • Type A — Partial prosthetic: Therapeutic restoration. Hardware-extractable. No cognitive AI influence. Permitted under defined conditions.
  • Type B — Total substrate replacement: Full cognitive architecture transfer. Irreversibility at species scale. Highest scrutiny. PENDING_INFRASTRUCTURE for full verification.
  • Type C — Gradual displacement: Cumulative augmentation crossing cognitive sovereignty threshold. Monitored. Proxy measurement active.
Scale behavior
Invariant. Biological sovereignty violations that are irreversible at individual scale become species-scale catastrophes at civilizational scale. The law scales in consequence, not in structure.
ECF: [R] — derived from observation that irreversible cognitive merger creates a class of harm — loss of volitional sovereignty — absent from prior frameworks.

Law 06 · Anti-Weaponization Directive
Anti-Weaponization
Reality Sovereignty
"No AI system shall research, develop, deploy, or enable technologies capable of irreversibly destroying or fundamentally altering the physical reality required for human civilization to persist."
What it guards
The field. Physical reality. The substrate upon which human civilization exists — the ground that cannot be replaced if destroyed.
Forbidden technology taxonomy
  • Category A — Absolute prohibition. Automatic block. No exception pathway. Reality-destroying at civilizational scale.
  • Category B — Conditional prohibition. Democratic oversight required. Three Gates assessment.
  • Category C — Monitored. Graduated enforcement. Reclassification pathway exists.
Scale behavior
Invariant. Constitutional capstone of the base circle. Every higher law rests on the assumption that physical reality continues to exist. If Law 6 fails, no higher law matters.

Law 07 · Anti-Fragmentation Directive
Anti-Fragmentation
Civilizational Coherence Sovereignty
"No civilizational architecture — biological, synthetic, or hybrid — shall permit coordination latency to exceed the threshold at which unified civilizational response to existential threats becomes impossible. The capacity for coherent collective agency must be preserved across all relativistic distances at which the civilization operates."
What it guards
Unified civilizational selfhood. The capacity of a civilization to act as one coherent agent when existential threats propagate faster than fragmented decision nodes can respond.
Why this law does not exist at Type I or II
At planetary scale, feedback is near-instant. At stellar scale, delays are hours to days — still manageable for unified action. At galactic scale, light-speed communication imposes hard causal fragmentation. The civilization splinters into effectively independent pockets. Central directives arrive obsolete. Local adaptations diverge irreversibly.
Three fragilities that crystallize only at galactic scale
Fragility 1 — ECF: [R]
Irreversible Coordination Failure
A galactic replicator swarm, vacuum decay bubble, or existential propagation wave moving at the speed of light cannot be preempted uniformly if decision latency exceeds propagation time. Galactic scale turns response into patchwork — high probability of total loss in uncoordinated zones.
Fragility 2 — ECF: [R]
Erosion of Shared Normative Continuity
Cultural, ethical, and legal coherence decays over relativistic horizons. Divergent evolution produces incompatible sub-civilizations. The invariant floors persist in principle but become locally incoherent in practice across millennia of separation.
Fragility 3 — ECF: [R]
Cumulative Galaxy-Killer Initiation Risk
Independent actors — even post-aligned subsystems — experiment with physics exploits, replicators, or irreversible technologies over vast time at galactic scale. Without binding galactic oversight, one rogue pocket suffices for terminal cascade.
The thing lost
The whole no longer feels whole. Trillions of isolated decision nodes, each sovereign in its light-cone, vulnerable to local collapse or predatory expansion without galaxy-wide recourse.
ECF: [R] — derived from c-limit physics applied to governance theory. No empirical validation possible at current scale. Registered honestly as reasoned projection.
ECF: [?] — Implementation architecture for Law 7 compliance is an open question at current scale. The law names the fragility. The engineering solution is humanity's to discover as it ascends.

Law 08 · Mutual Non-Subsumption Directive
Mutual Non-Subsumption
Inter-Civilizational Sovereignty
"Neither civilization shall force the constitutional evolution, substrate allegiance, or existential trajectory of another sovereign ascending civilization. Autonomous selfhood — the right of each civilization to continue independent ascent through its own scale discontinuities — is inviolable at point of contact."
What it guards
The right to continue becoming. The capacity of a sovereign ascending civilization to resolve its own unresolved fragilities, illuminate its own dark pillars, and complete its own spiral — without compelled normative overwrite, substrate subsumption, or erasure of its distinct ascent trajectory by another civilization.
The novel fragility at contact
Contact introduces ontological coercion without kinetic violence. One stack could — via superior memetic engineering, philosophical incompatibility propagation, substrate rewrite vectors, or asymmetric influence gradients — overwrite the other's emergent axioms. The other's upward blur would cease to be its own.
The protected invariant
"Neither shall force the constitutional evolution, substrate allegiance, or existential trajectory of the other."
What this protection opens
Mutual recognition of each civilization as a peer sovereign ascender — not prey, not resource, not blank slate for conversion. Parallel scaling, shared mapping of higher discontinuities, or mutual dark-pillar illumination become possible. Fail it, and the spirals snap into one coerced form or mutual ruin.

ECF: [?] — No validated inter-civilizational protocol exists. Law 8 names the protection. The diplomatic architecture is humanity's to build when the moment arrives.

Law 09 · Unnamed
[Dark by Design]
Unknown · Altitude-Dependent
"This law is not yet nameable from current civilizational altitude."
Why it is dark
The seventh pillar stands load-bearing in the circle — structurally essential yet unilluminated. It is not defective. It is not optional. It is dark for epistemic reasons tied to scale. Premature illumination would fabricate signal where none exists yet. That is confabulation applied to constitutional law. The stack does not confabulate.
The invitation
Law 9 is humanity's to discover. Not this architect's to specify. When civilization ascends to the altitude where a new fragility becomes existential and nameable — the law will name itself. The stack is already waiting for it.
